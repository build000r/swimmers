# ThrongTerm FAQ

ThrongTerm is a mobile terminal manager. It lets you run multiple shell sessions on a remote server from your phone.

**What are the little creatures?**
Those are Thronglets. Each one represents a terminal session. They wander around the field, and you tap one to open its terminal.

**How do I create a session?**
Long press anywhere on the field. A new Thronglet will appear and a terminal session starts on the server.

**How do I open a terminal?**
Tap a Thronglet. It opens a full-screen terminal connected to that session.

**How do I get back to the overview?**
Tap the back arrow in the top-left corner of the terminal view.

**What happens if I close the browser?**
Your sessions keep running on the server. When you come back, your Thronglets reappear with everything intact.

**Can I delete a session?**
Not yet from the UI — sessions persist until the server cleans them up or you kill them manually.
